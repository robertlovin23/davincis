About Us

DaVinci's Italian Ristorante open June 16th, 2010. Since that date, DaVinci's has been serving the best, most authentic Italian food in Stillwater. Our dishes are based on Italian recipes passed down from generation to generation. We specialize in traditional meat, seafood and pasta dishes as well as our hand-made pizzas. When you're looking for the best Italian in the Stillwater area, don't expect a national chain restaurant to exceed your expectations. We have been selected as Stillwater's Best Italian Restaurant by the NewsPress Readers' Choice Awards every year since our opening. We hope to serve you very soon. Grazie!
